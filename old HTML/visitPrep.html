Preparing for a visit is easy!

Middle School Preparation:

  1. Be sure that students are equipped with pencils
  2. Plan to be in the room during the presentation
  3. Have a white board and markers ready

High School Preparation:

  1. Have students brainstorm questions that they'd like to ask the presenters after the presentation. Ex: What is being a college student like? What was your favorite computer science project?

We'll bring copies and all lesson materials to your classroom. In general, not much preparation is necessary on the teacher's end.
